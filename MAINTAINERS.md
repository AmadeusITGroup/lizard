## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues
- Maintaining project roadmap and direction
- Ensuring code quality and test coverage
- Managing releases

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have demonstrated sustained, high-quality contributions may be invited to become maintainers. The process is:

1. Consistent contributions over a sustained period
2. Nomination by an existing maintainer
3. Approval by the current maintainer team
